IntelSeq

This app allows you to generate short loops of music. Instructions:

  • Click randomize to generate a sequence (shortkey: r)
  • Click play to play the result (shortkey: p)
  • Click stop to stop playing (shortkey: s)
Variables:
  • chord
  • tempo
  • MIDI ouptut (melody)
  • MIDI ouptut (drums)
  • MIDI ouptut (bass)
